Extra Options

Optional season toggles — customise your experience.
Non-Elimination Episodes Once per season (eps 5–7), the bottom 2 lip sync for the win instead of going home. No one is eliminated.
LaLaPaRuza Smackdown A mid-season lip sync smackdown (ep 8) where eliminated queens battle to re-enter the competition.
Due to Exiting Production (DEPT) Rarely (1% chance per elimination episode), a queen may be forced to withdraw due to injury. The bottom 2 still lip sync, but no one goes home. Regular Season only.
Disqualification (DISQ) Very rarely (0.5% chance per elimination episode), a queen may be disqualified for breaking competition rules and asked to leave immediately. The bottom 2 still lip sync normally. Regular Season only — not available on premieres, LaLaPaRuza, or Non-Elim episodes.
Queen Quits (QUIT) Rarely (2% chance per elimination episode), a queen announces during critiques that she wants to quit. She sashays away immediately. The episode then converts to a Top 2 format — the top two queens lip sync for the win, and all bottom queens receive a LOW placement instead. Only once per season. Regular Season only.
Regular Season Only
Returning Format Choose whether an eliminated queen returns to the competition during the season. Random Return: one eliminated queen is chosen randomly. Queens Vote: the remaining queens each vote for who they want to return — if there's a tie, you decide! Choose Who Returns: you personally pick which eliminated queen comes back. Cannot happen on premiere, Top 2, LaLaPaRuza, Final, or QUIT/DISQ/DEPT episodes. Queens who quit or were disqualified are not eligible. Regular Season only.
